# 👋 Welcome

**Monkey In Space Music Tech** is the technology division of **Monkey In Space**, created to develop modern, scalable, and innovative solutions for the music industry.

Our goal is to build our own technological infrastructure capable of connecting artists, labels, publishers, distributors, and companies across the sector through software, automation, metadata, and data intelligence.

More than just building tools, we aim to develop a complete ecosystem that contributes to the digital transformation of the music market.

---

# 🎯 Our Mission

To develop technology that simplifies processes, increases operational efficiency, and drives innovation in the music industry.
